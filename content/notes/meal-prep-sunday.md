# Sunday Meal Prep

## Chicken & Rice Bowls (5 portions)
- 1kg chicken breast, grilled
- 3 cups brown rice
- Roasted broccoli & sweet potato
- Sauce: soy + honey + garlic

## Overnight Oats (5 portions)
- 1/2 cup oats per jar
- 1 cup almond milk
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- Berries + banana on top

## Prep Timeline
1. Start rice (30 min)
2. Season & grill chicken (25 min)
3. Roast veggies (25 min)
4. Assemble oats (10 min)
5. Portion into containers
